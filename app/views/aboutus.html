About

The Southwest Fare Saver web app was created to supplement Southwest's website.

Refund Scanning

The app will scan southwest flight prices ever 6 hours. Once you input your flight information, an email will be sent to you if the price goes down. Additionally, you can watch how the flight price changes over time. Check out this article explaining how to get your money or points back if the price of your flight goes down: Refund Instructions

Another way to use this tool is to input flights that you are considering buying but are too expensive. Just set the cost of your flight to the price you are willing to pay and you will receive an email if that flight's price drops to your desired cost.

Automatic Check-In

This feature is under development.

If you have input your confirmation number, the app will also automatically check you in to your flight, excactly 24 hours before the flight.
