About Us

The Southwest Fare Saver web app was created to supplement Southwest's website. The app will check for refunds on flights you have already booked to see if they get any cheaper. Current Southwest policy allows you to rebook a flight and keep the difference in price. The app will also automatically check you in for flights, excactly 24 hours before the flight so you can get at the front of the "A" boarding group
