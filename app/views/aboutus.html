About

Dragon Fare Scanner was created to supplement Southwest Airline's website. Southwest famously doesn't share their prices on Kayak or almost any other airfare search or alerting sites.

Fare Scanner

Input your southwest flight information and the Dragon will periodically scan flight prices. An email will be sent to you if the price goes down and you can watch how the flight price changes over time. Check out this article explaining how to get your money or points back if the price of your flight goes down: Refund Instructions

Another way to use this app is to input flights that you are considering buying but are too expensive. Just set the cost of your flight to the price you are willing to pay and you will receive an email if that flight's price drops to your desired cost.
